About the project
Smart Mirror is our 5th year semester 9 project at Polytech Orléans. It represents more than a hundred hours of work.

In autonomy, we created and worked on our project sharing the work between us two (see section "The Team"). Starting by writing the specifications and create the design, the team did everything on this projet. It includes: mechanics, electronics, IT and communication.

What we do at Polytech Orléans

Imagine, organize, develop, optimize, control and test physical, electronic and innovative systems. We make them smart and communicative.
This base allows us to master the basic tools of engineering in Electronics, Computer Science and Optics, from the sensor, its electronics, its intelligence to its transmission and its connection with a cloud or a smartphone.
In addition, we develop a number of business skills such as Lighting, Connected Objects and Augmented Reality for Vision, topics that are very popular today and can be used from the very beginning.
